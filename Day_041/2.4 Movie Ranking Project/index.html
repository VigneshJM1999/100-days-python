The Best Movies According to Vignesh

My top 3 movies of all times

Thani Oruvan

Mithran, a police officer, pledges to reveal the lies and malpractices of Siddharth Abhimanyu, a renowned scientist, who uses illegal methods to make profit.

Vikram Vedha

A neo-noir crime thriller that cleverly blurs the lines between good and evil.

Kaithi

An ex-convict's fresh start to life is threatened by vengeful enemies, compelling him to confront the dangers for a chance at freedom.
